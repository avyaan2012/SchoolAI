START HERE

1. Open SETUP.md.
2. Copy app/, components/, and lib/ into your existing Next.js project.
3. Run: npm install @google/genai
4. Add GEMINI_API_KEY to .env.local and Vercel.
5. Merge the example from examples/QuizResultCard.example.jsx into your
   current quiz-results component.
6. Connect lib/resources/projectResourceAdapter.js to your school-resource
   database when ready.

Do not upload this whole folder as a separate website. Merge its contents
into your existing repository.
